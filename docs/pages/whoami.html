EXPLANATION

LAWA MENA HAU LALA
"Keep moving forward, even if it means shedding blood."

• LAWA - to navigate or advance
• MENA - with purposeful direction
• HAU - spirit or burning passion
• LALA - blood

"LAWA MENA HAU LALA," a powerful expression from the Maluku region of Indonesia, embodies courage, persistence, and unwavering determination. It is a call to advance with bravery and resilience, no matter the cost.

As someone passionate about computer science and actively searching for an Apprenticeship (for 2026), I admire the Willpower and Passion from around the world, like from the Moluccan people. Historically, this phrase was used during times of struggle and resistance, including the fight against colonial powers.
